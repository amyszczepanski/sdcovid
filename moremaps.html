A few more maps about COVID-19 in San Diego County

  • Orange colors for ZIP codes with 5 or more cases and population at least 10,000.
  • Grayscale for ZIP codes with fewer than 5 cases or population less than 10,000.
  • More intense color means more cases.
  • Each frame represents one day and is 1/5 of a second.
  • There is a 4 second pause before the animation loops.
  • Right click to download or copy the GIF.
  • The GIFs update themselves in the middle of the night.
